Copyright 2020 Joel Ryerson • Product Designer

My mission is to collaborate on future environments that promote accessibility, inclusivity, & equality.

I have a passion for understanding human behavior and value empathy, curiosity, and humility. On good days and bad I strive to be 100% authentic and honest with myself and others.

Working in many different fields, I’ve formed relationships that have enabled me to understand human interaction on a deeper, more intimate level. Feeling the struggle or joy of another opens the door to compassion and understanding. Without this critical part of design, you have fool’s gold.

To articulate my approach, I give equal focus on human centered design and engaging visual components. Collaborating with an open, honest, and positive mindset helps me quickly adapt when a project inevitably takes new or unexpected turns. I love being involved in the entire process, starting with ideation and definition, to prototyping, testing, and delivering the final iteration.
